Uniben Fagcoop MPCS Ltd

Website Planning Document

Site Name

UNIBEN FAGCOOP MPCS Ltd – This name represents a financial cooperative society based in University of Benin, Benin City, Nigeria. It was chosen to highlight the community-centered mission of promoting financial inclusion and empowerment.

Optional domain availability: https:/fagcoopmpcs.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Uniben Faculty of Agriculture Staff Multi-Purpose Cooperative Society Limited (FAGCOOP) website is to provide information about the cooperative’s mission, membership benefits, savings and loan services, community development initiatives, and contact details. It will serve as a hub for members and prospective members to learn, engage, and access financial services.

Scenarios

  • How can I join the cooperative and what are the membership requirements?
  • What savings and loan options are available to members?
  • Where can I find updates on community development projects?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Deep Blue (#004466) – Used for headings, header background, and borders.

Accent Color: Orange (#ff6600) – Used for logo and highlights.

Background Color: Light Gray (#f9f9f9) – Used for page background.

Typography

Headings: Georgia, serif – Professional and trustworthy look.

Body Text: Arial, sans-serif – Clean and easy to read for informational content.

Planned Website Sections

  • About Us: History and purpose of the cooperative.
  • Membership: How to join, requirements, and benefits.
  • Services: Savings plans, loan options, and financial advisory support.
  • Community Impact: Projects and contributions to local development.
  • News & Updates: Announcements, financial literacy tips, and events.
  • Gallery: Photos showcasing community projects and member activities.
  • Contact: Office location, phone numbers, and online inquiry form.

Reason for Choosing This Subject

I chose this subject because cooperative societies play a vital role in promoting financial inclusion and community empowerment in Nigeria. By creating a responsive website, the cooperative can reach more people, provide easy access to information, and strengthen trust among members. It also reflects my interest in financial literacy and how digital tools can support local communities.
