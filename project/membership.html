Join Our Cooperative Family

At Benin City Cooperative Society, membership means more than financial services. It’s about belonging to a community that values growth, support, and shared success.

Membership Benefits

  • Access to Savings & Loans: Secure and affordable financial solutions tailored to members.
  • Financial Literacy Workshops: Learn practical skills to manage money wisely.
  • Community Projects: Be part of initiatives that uplift our neighborhoods.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with professionals and entrepreneurs in Benin City.

Membership Requirements

To become a member, you need:

  1. A valid ID card (National ID, Voter’s Card, or Passport).
  2. Proof of residence in Benin City.
  3. Completed membership application form.
  4. Payment of the one-time registration fee.

How to Join

You can apply in two ways:

📍 In-Person

Visit our office at FAGCOOOP Secretariat Building, Opposite June 12 Building, University of Benin, Benin City to fill out the form.

🌐 Online

Complete the Online Membership Form and submit your documents digitally.
